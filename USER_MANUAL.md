# 🌉 InternLink: Platform Navigation Guide (v1.0)
## High-Fidelity UI/UX Operations Manual

Welcome to the **InternLink** (Version 1.0) Operation Manual. This guide is built based on the actual frontend architecture to ensure you can navigate the platform with precision.

---

## 🏗️ The Global Interface
InternLink utilizes a **Minimalist Fintech Design** system characterized by:
*   **White Sidebar (Navigation)**: Your primary command center.
*   **Minimalist Topbar**: Shows your profile, notifications, and global search.
*   **Accent Theme**: Soft-purple and Teal active states for clear visual hierarchy.

---

## 👨‍🎓 For Students: Securing Your Placement

### 1. The Home Dashboard
Upon login, you land on the **Home Dashboard**. 
*   **Search Console**: Located at the top. Search by role title (e.g., "Frontend Developer"), company name, or specific skills.
*   **Mode Filters**: Quickly toggle between **Remote**, **Hybrid**, or **On-site** opportunities using the chip navigation.

### 2. Browsing Opportunities
The central feed displays active internship nodes. 
*   **Quick Scan**: Each card shows the duration, location, and monthly stipend (e.g., "$2k/mo").
*   **Deep View**: Click any card to expand the **Detail Modal**. Here you can read the "About the Role" section and specific requirements.
*   **The Action**: Click **Apply Now** at the bottom of any detail modal. The system will instantly transmit your profile to the employer.

### 3. Tracking Progress
Use the sidebar to access your profile and settings. Your **Home Dashboard** will always hide roles you've already applied for, keeping your feed fresh.

## 🏢 For Employers: Recruitment Operations

### 1. The Recruitment Hub (Dashboard)
Your dashboard is a data-dense command center.
*   **Metric Chips**: High-level counters for total **Applications**, **Active Interns**, and **Open Roles**.
*   **Initiate Posting**: Click the **Post New Role** card to open the "New Posting" modal. You only need the Title, Description, Skills, and a Deadline.
*   **Watchlist**: A live feed of the latest applicants, including their **AI Match %**—a calculated score helping you prioritize high-fit candidates.

### 2. Managing the Pipeline
Navigate to **Applicants** in the sidebar for granular control.
*   **Filtering**: Use the dropdown at the top to filter applicants by a specific job listing.
*   **The Action Row**: 
    *   Click the **Green Check (UserCheck)** icon to hire a candidate instantly.
    *   Click the **Red X (UserX)** icon to decline an applicant.
    *   Click the **Arrow Icon** to view the candidate's full resume/CV.

## 🛠️ For System Admins: Global Governance

### 1. System Overview
The Super Admin dashboard provides a macroscopic view of the entire ecosystem.
*   **KPI Metrics**: Monitor total Partnerships and Placements in real-time.
*   **Population Distribution**: A donut chart visualizing the ratio of Interns to other system roles.
*   **Audit Log**: A list of recent system activities. Look for the "Outcome" column—**Verified** items are successful, while **Flagged** items indicate potential issues.

### 2. User Provisioning
Navigate to **Users** to manage the platform registry.
*   **Add New User**: Click the **Add New User** button to open the "Provisioning Modal".
*   **Governance**: 
    *   **Suspend**: Use the **UserX** icon to temporarily block a user's access without deleting their data.
    *   **Restore**: Use the **UserCheck** icon to re-activate a suspended account.
    *   **Delete**: Use the **Trash** icon for permanent removal.

## 💡 Pro-Tips for all Users

> [!TIP]
> **Mobile Optimization**: InternLink is fully responsive. On mobile devices, use the **Bottom Navigation Bar** for quick access to your most-used panels.

> [!IMPORTANT]
